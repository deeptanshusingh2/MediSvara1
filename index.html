About MediSvara

Mental health challenges like stress, anxiety, and loneliness are increasingly common among higher education students. Many struggle in silence due to lack of accessible support, stigma, or busy academic schedules.

MediSvara bridges this gap by providing a student-centered, lightweight, and community-driven platform that combines self-help tools with human connection in one seamless experience.
